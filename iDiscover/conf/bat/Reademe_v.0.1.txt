iTransformer is simple but powerful open product for network transformation licensed under GPLv.3.

This is the initial Release of iTransformer. It's sole purpose is to fulfill the needs of some impatient users.
It is sufficient for discovering IP networks through SNMP and for previewing and analysing the discovered network topology.

Watch out for the next version. It's main purpose is to include features such as discovering and previewing BGP Internet Map.
